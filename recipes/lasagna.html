Lasagna

Original family lasagna made with love. This recipe takes time, but it's all worth it!

Ingredients

  • Grind meat
  • Lasagna sheets
  • Tomato puree
  • Vegetable stock
  • 2 carrots
  • 1 onion
  • 2 cloves of garlic

Step by step

  1. Cut the onion, carrots, and garlic
  2. Put all the cut veggies into a pan
  3. Add grind meat
